The Story

The body is a complex machine and ten years ago I suspected mine was out of tune. I’ve been on hunt for remedies to my own foibles and ailments. Somewhat out of necessity I’ve become a health geek and self-experimenter trying to deal with real cognitive problems affecting by energy and happiness. So this chronicles how I’m getting “back to normal” not pushing myself to the optimal edge for seemingly trivial things. This is an important distinction that separates the geeks (a.k.a. me) from the self-help gurus.

The interesting thing about society today is it’s anything but normal. There is an evolutionary disconnect between our biology and the expectations of the 21st century. So I suspect I’ve been through what some of you are also going through, which is why I’d like to share these experiments and give you a front row seat to follow the breadcrumbs.

Join me on my continued journey of uncovering the body’s many hidden levers – and experimenting my way to mastering them.
